- Simplified icon version

‚ùå Don't stretch or distort

## üó£Ô∏è Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Characteristics:**

**Professional yet Approachable**
- Use clear, jargon-free language
- Be helpful and informative
- Maintain credibility

**Confident but Humble**
- Show expertise without arrogance
- Acknowledge when we don't know
- Always learning and improving

**Inspiring and Optimistic**
- Focus on possibilities
- Encourage exploration
- Celebrate achievements

### Tone Guidelines

**Writing for Different Contexts:**

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Marketing** | Exciting, inspiring | "Transform your tourism business with AI-powered tools" |
| **Documentation** | Clear, instructive | "Follow these steps to integrate the API" |
| **Error Messages** | Helpful, apologetic | "We couldn't save your changes. Please try again." |
| **Success Messages** | Celebratory, friendly | "Great! Your content is now published." |
| **Support** | Empathetic, solution-focused | "We understand this is frustrating. Here's how we'll fix it." |

### Writing Best Practices

‚úÖ **Be Concise** - Respect user's time  
‚úÖ **Use Active Voice** - More direct and engaging  
‚úÖ **Focus on Benefits** - "You can" not "The system allows"  
‚úÖ **Be Inclusive** - Avoid jargon, consider global audience  
‚úÖ **Proofread** - Check spelling, grammar, tone

## üéØ Design Principles

### 1. **User-Centric**
Design for the user first, not for ourselves. Understand their needs, context, and goals.

### 2. **Clarity Over Creativity**
When in doubt, choose clarity. Users should never have to guess.

### 3. **Consistent & Predictable**
Similar things should look and behave similarly across the platform.

### 4. **Progressive Disclosure**
Show only what's necessary, when it's necessary. Don't overwhelm.

### 5. **Accessible by Default**
Design for everyone, including those with disabilities.

### 6. **Mobile-First**
Start with mobile, scale up. Most users are on mobile devices.

### 7. **Performance Matters**
Fast is a feature. Optimize for speed at every step.

## üåç Global Considerations

### RTL Support

**For Arabic and Hebrew:**
- Mirror layouts horizontally
- Text alignment: right
- Icons: mirrored where directional
- Maintain logical reading order

### Internationalization

- Use Unicode-compatible fonts
- Leave space for text expansion (30-50%)
- Use locale-appropriate date/time formats
- Consider cultural color meanings
